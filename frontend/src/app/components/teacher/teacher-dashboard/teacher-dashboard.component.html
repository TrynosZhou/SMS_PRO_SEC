⚡ What you can do here

This portal shows your classes, subjects, and tools — not whole-school statistics. Use the sidebar or shortcuts below for exams, attendance, report cards, and more.

  • Enter and review marks for assessments you’re involved in.
  • Use the Record Book, Create Task, and Submissions under E-learning Services.
  • Take attendance and view attendance reports for your groups.
  • View or generate report cards where permitted.
  • Review rankings for your subjects or classes.
  • Browse student profiles you’re allowed to see.
